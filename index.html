About Me

Hi, I'm Utsav Bhandari — a graphic designer and visual artist from the hills of Sindhuli, Nepal. I discovered my love for design when I was just 16 years old, and what started as curiosity quickly grew into a calling. At 21, I bring over 6 years of hands-on experience to every project I touch.

My journey began in my hometown, where I started designing logos, posters, and branding materials for small local businesses — shops, general stores, and restaurants that needed a professional visual identity without the big-city price tag. Seeing my work bring their businesses to life, and getting paid for something I love, was the fuel that kept me going.

Today I specialize in t-shirt graphic design, logo creation, poster design, and brand identity. My style blends bold typography with strong visual hierarchy — designs that communicate instantly and stay memorable long after first glance.

I'm proud of every design I've created because each one carries a piece of someone's dream — and my craft. Whether you're a startup, a local business, or just someone with a creative idea, I'm here to bring it to life visually.
